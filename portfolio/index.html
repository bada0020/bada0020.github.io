About me

My name is Dima Badawi and I'm a current Graphic Design student based in Ottawa, ON. I come from an interesting mix of Lebanese parents who decided to raise all 6 of their kids in Dubai, U.A.E. and then moved to Canada 5 years ago.

The quote you saw when you landed here is one I hold close to heart. I am a silly Graphic Designer who dreams of landing on the moon. As a Graphic Designer, I have accepted that the rest of my life from here on will consist of solving other people’s problems and miserably failing at solving mine. Now that you know how hard I’m willing to work, take a look at my portfolio and contact me if your willing to do the impossible.
